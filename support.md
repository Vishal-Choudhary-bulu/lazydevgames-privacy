## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I report a bug?
Please email us at help@lazydevgames.com with details about the issue, including:
- Which app you're using
- What you were doing when the issue occurred
- Your macOS version

### How do I request a feature?
Send us an email at help@lazydevgames.com with your feature request. We'd love to hear your ideas!

### Is my data safe?
Yes! We don't collect any data. See our [Privacy Policy](index.md) for details.
