# Privacy Policy for Lazy's Image Tools

**Last Updated: December 27, 2025**

## Overview
Lazy's Image Tools is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data collection and usage.

## Data Collection
**We collect NO data whatsoever.**

Specifically, we do NOT collect, store, transmit, or process:
- Personal information
- Usage data or analytics
- Crash reports
- Image files or content
- User preferences or settings
- Device information
- Location data
- Any other user data

## How The App Works
- All image processing occurs entirely on your local device
- No images are uploaded to any server
- No network connections are made for app functionality
- No user accounts or registration required
- No tracking or analytics tools are integrated

## Image Processing
- All images you process remain on your Mac
- Images are only accessed through macOS file selection dialogs
- Processed images are saved only to locations you specify
- No copies are retained by the application

## App Sandbox
This app runs in Apple's App Sandbox environment, which restricts access to:
- Network connections (not used)
- Files outside your explicit selection
- System resources beyond basic image processing

## Third-Party Services
We use NO third-party services, including:
- No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations

## Data Storage
The app stores only:
- User preferences (window size, last used settings)
- These are stored locally in macOS standard locations
- Can be deleted by removing the app

## Children's Privacy
Our app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Your Rights
Since we collect no data, there is no data to:
- Access
- Modify
- Delete
- Export

## Changes to This Policy
If our privacy practices change, we will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy:
- Email: contact@lazydevgames.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/lazydevgames/lazydevgames-privacy

## Compliance
This app complies with:
- Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

**Summary: We built a tool that respects your privacy completely. Your images and data never leave your Mac.**
